About Nerds Gummy Clusters

Nerds Gummy Clusters are a sweet, chewy candy made by Nestle's Nerds brand, offering a unique combination of tangy candy coating and chewy gummy center. They are any candy lover's dream all in one

Fun Facts about Nerds Gummy Clusters

  • First introduced in 2019 by the popular Nerds brand.
  • The candy is available in a variety of vibrant, fruity flavors like Tropical and Wild Berry.
  • Nerds Gummy Clusters are made with no artificial flavors, giving them a delicious natural taste.
  • The candy is loved by people of all ages, and the chewy gummy centers make them a great treat for those who enjoy both gummy and crunchy textures!
